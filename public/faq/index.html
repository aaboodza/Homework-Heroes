Our offered courses are completely free of charge. Enroll into the one you are interested in with just one click of a button, and gain access to all our free lessons and assessments.

No prior experience is needed in any of our courses. All our lessons start with the basic fundamentals before gradually increasing in difficulty. Choose the lessons you are most comfortable with, and test yourselves with the included assessments.

We are a one-stop learning platform for all your learning needs. We offer a variety of programs from coding to biology, all with different learning outcomes. As of now, we provide courses to the three main building blocks of the web, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

We do not give any certifications for completing a course, as all of our courses offered are free of charge.

None of our courses have a deadline. Choose what time works best for you, and set your own due dates with the built in calendar.
